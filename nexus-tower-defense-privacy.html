Privacy Policy

Nexus Tower Defense
Package: com.eduardhirsch.ehnexustd
Developer: Eduard Hirsch
Effective date: 15 May 2026

This policy describes how Nexus Tower Defense ("the app", "we") handles information when you use the Android version distributed on Google Play. The app is a single-player tower defense game. We do not operate user accounts or social features in the current release.

Summary

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not require sign-in to play.
  • Gameplay progress is stored locally on your device.
  • The app does not send gameplay or personal data to our servers in the current release.

Information stored on your device

The app saves game settings and progress (for example scores, unlocks, and preferences) in private app storage on your device. This data stays on your device unless you clear app data, uninstall the app, or use a device backup feature provided by your phone manufacturer or Google.

We do not receive this local save data unless you choose to contact us and share information voluntarily (for example in a support email).

Network access

The app declares the INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE (network state) permissions on Android. In the current release, the game is playable offline and we do not use these permissions to collect personal information or to upload your save data to our servers.

Android or Google Play may use network access on the device for store-related functions when you install or update the app through Google Play. That processing is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

What we do not collect

In the current release we do not collect:

  • Name, email address, or phone number through the app
  • Precise location
  • Contacts, photos, or files outside the app's private storage
  • Advertising identifiers (no ads SDK in this release)
  • Analytics or crash-reporting identifiers through third-party SDKs

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Data retention and deletion

Local game data remains on your device until you remove it. You can delete it by clearing the app's storage in Android settings or uninstalling the app.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page when the app changes (for example if we add online features, ads, or account login). We will post the new effective date at the top. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.
